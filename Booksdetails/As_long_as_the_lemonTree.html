Book Description :

As Long as the Lemon Trees Grow is a gripping young adult novel set during the Syrian civil war.
It tells the story of Salama Kassab, a pharmacy student turned volunteer at a hospital in Homs.
As the violence intensifies, Salama faces an impossible choice: escape Syria to protect herself and her family, or stay and help those in need.
