The Silent Patient

Alex Michaelides

Book Description :

The Silent Patient is a gripping psychological thriller centered around Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who shockingly shoots her husband and then stops speaking entirely.
Her silence turns the case into a national mystery. Enter Theo Faber, a criminal psychotherapist who becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind Alicia’s silence. As he digs deeper into her life and mind, dark secrets begin to unravel — leading to a jaw-dropping twist. The novel explores themes of trauma, obsession, mental health, and the unreliability of perception.
